# A-Data-Driven-Analysis-of-Sleep-Patterns-Lifestyle-Factors-and-Health-Impacts-in-Bangladesh
A comprehensive data analytics project focused on understanding sleep behavior in Bangladesh. The study leverages exploratory data analysis and visualization to identify key patterns, correlations, and health impacts associated with lifestyle factors.

---
📌 Project Overview
---
This project presents a data-driven analysis of sleep patterns in Bangladesh, focusing on how lifestyle habits influence sleep behavior and their impact on physical and mental health. The study uses exploratory data analysis (EDA) and visualization techniques to uncover meaningful insights from the dataset.

---
🧾 Project Statement
---
Sleep patterns are closely linked to lifestyle habits and health outcomes, yet limited data-driven insights exist for populations in Bangladesh. This study aims to analyze sleep behavior, identify influencing lifestyle factors, and understand their impact on physical and mental health.

---
🎯 Objectives
---
📊 Analyze sleep patterns among individuals

🧹 Perform data cleaning and preprocessing

🔍 Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)

📱 Identify key factors contributing to late sleeping

😴 Evaluate the health impacts of poor sleep

📈 Visualize data using charts and graphs

💡 Derive meaningful insights from the dataset

---
📂 Dataset Description
---
- Dataset Name: Survey Dataset on Sleep Patterns, Health Effects, and Lifestyle Factors in Bangladesh
- Source URL: [View Dataset](https://example.com/dataset)

📊 Key Features of the Dataset
---
👥 Demographics: Age range, gender,height,weight,occupation

⏰ Sleep Schedule: Bedtime and wake-up time (weekday/weekend)

😴 Sleep Quality: Duration and time taken to fall asleep 

⚠️ Sleep Disturbances: Breathing issues, restlessness,medical conditions  

📱 Behavioral Factors: Reason for staying up late(Social media, stress, study/work)

💡 Health Impacts: Side effect of poor sleep(eg.Fatigue, lack of concentration) 

☕ Coping Strategies: Methods used to manage sleep deprivation(eg.Caffeine, naps)  

🏠 Environment: Self-rated Comfort of the sleeping envirorment(1–5 scale)

📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
---
Key analyses performed:

- Univariate analysis (distribution of sleep hours, side effects,reasons)
- Bivariate analysis (sleep vs lifestyle, health vs sleep)
- (Multivariate analysis (sleep duration, latency, and lifestyle factors vs health impacts)
- Correlation analysis (heatmaps)
- Weekday vs weekend sleep comparison
- Sleep latency and health condition relationships

---
🔍 Key Insights
---
- Screen exposure and stress are major contributors to late sleep
- Most individuals sleep between 6–8 hours, but irregular patterns exist
- Common side effects include fatigue, health issues, and mood swings
- Poor sleep significantly impacts concentration and productivity
- Weekend sleep patterns differ noticeably from weekdays
